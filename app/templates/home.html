AI Application Compiler

Build apps with a compiler pipeline, not a one-shot prompt.

This demo turns natural language into a typed application spec, validates it across layers, repairs only the broken parts, and then emits a real runnable workspace. The point is reliability and engineering discipline, not flashy text generation.
